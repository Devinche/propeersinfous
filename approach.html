How we work

How a project actually goes.

No mystery, no ten-week discovery phase before anything ships. Here is the shape of a typical engagement.

Discover

We figure out what you're trying to do and what's in the way, then agree on what a win looks like before anyone writes code.

Design & build

A small senior team builds in short cycles and shows you working software early, not three months later when it's too late to change course.

Launch

We ship it and hand it over cleanly, with tracking in place so you can see exactly what it's doing in the real world.

Grow

Then support keeps it healthy and the affiliate side keeps it earning long after the launch is done.

What working with us feels like

You get one main point of contact who actually knows the work, not an account manager relaying messages to a team you never meet. Updates come on a regular cadence and they show real progress, not status colors on a slide.

We price in a way you can plan around, and we flag scope changes when they happen instead of at the invoice. If we hit something that should change the plan, you hear about it the same week, with options.

When the work is done

You walk away owning everything. The code, the accounts, the documentation, all of it. If you want us to stay on for support and growth we are happy to, but it is your choice and not a trap baked into the build.
