Practice 01

IT Consulting

Strategy plus hands-on building for teams that need their tech to actually work when it counts.

Your tech should be something you stop thinking about, because it just works. That is the job. We look at what you are running, then either fix what is slowing you down or build the thing you keep putting off.

We have done this across a lot of stacks and a lot of industries, so we tend to spot the real problem before you finish describing it. You get senior people who have shipped before, not a rotating cast of juniors learning on your budget.

What's included
  • Cloud migration and DevOps setup
  • Custom and enterprise software builds
  • Managed IT and day-to-day support
  • Legacy system modernization
  • Third-party and API integration
  • Security reviews and hardening

How an engagement runs

We start small. A short discovery to understand your systems and what you are trying to reach, then a plan with real numbers attached. From there a focused team gets to work in short cycles, and you see progress every week rather than waiting months for a big reveal. When it is done we hand it over cleanly, with documentation your own people can follow.

If something is outside what we should be doing, we will say so. The goal is for you to trust the advice even when it points away from a bigger invoice.
