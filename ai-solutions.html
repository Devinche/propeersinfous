AI Platforming & Solutions

Practical AI that makes it into production instead of dying in a pilot.

Most companies do not need a research lab. They need one or two AI features that save real time and hold up once actual users get their hands on them. That is what we build.

We start from a workflow you already have, find the spot where a model genuinely helps, and ship something you can put in front of people. If a pilot cannot make it past the demo stage, we tell you early instead of billing you to find out the hard way.

What's included
  • LLM apps and chat assistants
  • Intelligent agents and automation
  • RAG and private knowledge search
  • Custom ML models and data pipelines
  • Evaluation and production monitoring
  • AI strategy and governance

How we keep it grounded

The hard part of AI work is not the demo. It is everything after: keeping answers accurate, watching cost per request, and knowing when the model is wrong. We wire in evaluation from the start so you can see whether a change made things better or just different. Your data stays yours, and we build on top of the systems you already run rather than asking you to rip them out.

We also tell you when AI is the wrong tool. Plenty of problems are solved better by plain software, and pretending otherwise just burns money.
